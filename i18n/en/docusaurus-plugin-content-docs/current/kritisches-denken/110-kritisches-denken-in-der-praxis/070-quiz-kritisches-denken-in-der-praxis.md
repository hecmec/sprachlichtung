## Quiz questions:

<!--t src=ea880607-->
### 1. **Which of the following is NOT a component of the critical thinking process?**

<!--t src=c05b3e0f-->
- a) Clear definition of the problem
- b) Gathering information
- c) Emotional reaction
- d) Alternative perspectives

<!--t src=971638cc-->
### 2. **Which tool is best suited to representing the structure of a complex argument visually?**

<!--t src=10916d03-->
- a) Pro-and-con table
- b) SWOT analysis
- c) Argument mapping
- d) Decision matrix

<!--t src=070fc7fb-->
### 3. **Which strategy is best suited to overcoming confirmation bias?**

<!--t src=e46028a5-->
- a) Practising emotional distancing
- b) Actively seeking out information that contradicts one's own beliefs
- c) Setting priorities and creating time for reflection
- d) Fostering psychological safety in groups

<!--t src=ef8eb7cc-->
### 4. **Which of the following is an external obstacle to critical thinking?**

<!--t src=c778ee94-->
- a) Cognitive biases
- b) Emotional barriers
- c) Echo chambers and filter bubbles
- d) Hasty judgement

<!--t src=494b3c0b-->
### 5. **Which principle is most important when setting goals for the development of critical thinking?**

<!--t src=b328fcee-->
- a) The goals should be as demanding as possible
- b) The goals should be specific and measurable
- c) The goals should be set by others
- d) The goals should focus on theoretical knowledge

<!--t src=aefcf85c-->
## Answers:

<!--t src=965e1271-->
1. c) Emotional reaction
2. c) Argument mapping
3. b) Actively seeking out information that contradicts one's own beliefs
4. c) Echo chambers and filter bubbles
5. b) The goals should be specific and measurable
