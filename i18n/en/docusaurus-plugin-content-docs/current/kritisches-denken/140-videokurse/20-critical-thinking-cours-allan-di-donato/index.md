## Course overview

Here we give an overview of Allan Di Donato's YouTube course on critical thinking. Di Donato is a philosophy professor at Central Piedmont Community College.

This course stands out for its breadth (all the important topics are covered) and its neutral tone. It is well made, entertaining, and well suited to beginners.

Allan Di Donato has worked extensively on Christian-biblical and mythological topics, but this preference enriches his course more than it compromises its neutrality.
