## Quiz questions:

<!--t src=afc31665-->
### 1. **Which manipulation technique is being used when a salesperson says: "Only 3 left in stock! Order now!"?**

<!--t src=e5802bbe-->
- a) Emotional manipulation
- b) Scarcity principle
- c) Appeal to authority
- d) Reciprocity

<!--t src=649d1219-->
### 2. **What is a characteristic feature of propaganda?**

<!--t src=6f256bb8-->
- a) Presenting various perspectives
- b) Promoting critical thinking
- c) Emotionalisation and oversimplification
- d) Transparent attribution of sources

<!--t src=dd386442-->
### 3. **Which phenomenon describes the situation in which algorithms personalise content based on earlier preferences, so that users increasingly see only information that confirms their existing views?**

<!--t src=718369af-->
- a) Astroturfing
- b) Deepfake
- c) Filter bubble
- d) Gaslighting

<!--t src=2bfb0cb6-->
### 4. **Which strategy is best suited to resisting emotional manipulation?**

<!--t src=e92742d9-->
- a) Triangulation of information
- b) Emotional awareness and detachment
- c) Active source-checking
- d) Diversification of information sources

<!--t src=9ab3310c-->
### 5. **Which of the following statements is an example of gaslighting?**

<!--t src=5bdfe45e-->
- a) "All experts recommend this product."
- b) "You're imagining it. I never said I would do that."
- c) "Only on offer today!"
- d) "The silent majority stands behind us."

<!--t src=aefcf85c-->
## Answers:

<!--t src=24aca0da-->
1. b) Scarcity principle
2. c) Emotionalisation and oversimplification
3. c) Filter bubble
4. b) Emotional awareness and detachment
5. b) "You're imagining it. I never said I would do that."
