<!--t src=9563a8da-->
:::tip In brief
What science does not (yet) explain is summarily attributed to a supernatural cause.

_Where knowledge ends, the assertion begins._
:::

<!--t src=23594b0b-->
## Definition

<!--t src=344e6dc9-->
The **god of the gaps** argument fills an **explanatory gap** in current knowledge with a supernatural cause: because something is not (yet) explained, an intervention by God (or some other ultimate authority) is assumed as the explanation.

<!--t src=712db7b1-->
The error: a gap in knowledge is no evidence for a particular explanation. „We don't know yet“ is not the same as „it must be supernatural“. Historically, such gaps have again and again been closed by natural explanations.

<!--t src=3c24b250-->
The fallacy has the following form:

<!--t src=d707e2d3-->
1. The phenomenon P is (currently) unexplained.
2. I cannot think of a natural explanation.
3. Therefore the cause of P is supernatural.

<!--t src=aa2d2a12-->
Also known as: _god of the gaps_ / _divine fallacy_

<!--t src=2851dcb3-->
## Related fallacies

<!--t src=4d08b756-->
<!-- list similar fallacies -->

<!--t src=2a24ab90-->
- **Personal incredulity** – the root: „I can't explain it to myself“ is turned into a conclusion.
- **Argumentum ad Ignorantiam** – the umbrella term: from not-knowing a particular thesis is inferred.
- **False dilemma** – related: pretending there is only „explained“ or „supernatural“.

## Examples

<!--t src=a67a7a61-->
### Example 1

<!--t src=5422e642-->
<!-- description of example one -->

<!--t src=e55b4d9a-->
> „Science cannot explain exactly how life arose – so God must have created it.“

<!--t src=5c90f8be-->
An open research question is no evidence for a particular answer. The gap is filled prematurely.

<!--t src=f350657d-->
### Example 2

<!--t src=c7d6981c-->
<!-- description of example two -->

<!--t src=f8fd93a1-->
> „No one knows why the natural constants have exactly these values – that can only be by design.“

<!--t src=2972d08a-->
From the lack of knowledge about the cause, no planning agency follows.
