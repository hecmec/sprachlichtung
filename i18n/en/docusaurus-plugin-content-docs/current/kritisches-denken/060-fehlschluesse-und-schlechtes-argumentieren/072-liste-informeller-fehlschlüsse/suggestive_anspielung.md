<!--t src=bf360141-->
:::tip In brief
A suspicion is aroused through a hint or question, without ever asserting or substantiating it.

_What is not said works the strongest._
:::

<!--t src=23594b0b-->
## Definition

<!--t src=1c416fbd-->
The **insinuation** (innuendo) produces a negative impression not through an open claim, but through **hints, questions or omissions**. A suspicion is thereby floated without one's spelling it out – and so without having to substantiate it.

<!--t src=c0570a92-->
The error: the hint smuggles in an unproven imputation. It exploits the fact that listeners fill the gap themselves with the suggested suspicion.

<!--t src=3c24b250-->
The fallacy has the following form:

<!--t src=86f317ee-->
1. It is suggested that something incriminating (E) holds about A.
2. E is never explicitly asserted – and therefore not substantiated either.
3. The audience nonetheless takes E to be implied.

<!--t src=ec6b8aa6-->
Also known as: _insinuation_ / _innuendo_

<!--t src=2851dcb3-->
## Related fallacies

<!--t src=4d08b756-->
<!-- list similar fallacies -->

<!--t src=2b0a5e7f-->
- **Poisoning the well** – related: mistrust against a person produced in advance.
- **Complex question** – related: an unproven assumption is lodged in a question.
- **Red herring** – related, when the insinuation steers away from the topic.

<!--t src=9dbba01b-->
## Examples

<!--t src=a67a7a61-->
### Example 1

<!--t src=5422e642-->
<!-- description of example one -->

<!--t src=66d18e11-->
> „Why don't you ask him sometime how he actually finances his election campaign.“

<!--t src=f86520fc-->
Nothing is asserted – and yet the suspicion of shady sources of money is aroused, entirely without evidence.

<!--t src=f350657d-->
### Example 2

<!--t src=c7d6981c-->
<!-- description of example two -->

<!--t src=3a441a46-->
> „I don't want to say anything, but it is striking how quickly she was promoted.“

<!--t src=0ea67799-->
The protestation of „not wanting to say anything“ carries precisely the imputation that is not spoken aloud.
