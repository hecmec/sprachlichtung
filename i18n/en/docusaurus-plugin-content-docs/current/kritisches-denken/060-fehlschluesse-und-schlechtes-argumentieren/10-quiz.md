## Quiz Questions:

<!--t src=303412cb-->
### 1. **Which fallacy is present when someone argues: "No one has proven that extraterrestrials do not exist, so they must exist"?**
   a) Post Hoc Ergo Propter Hoc
   b) Argumentum ad Ignorantiam
   c) Petitio Principii
   d) False dichotomy

<!--t src=ae6337ef-->
### 2. **A politician says: "My opponent smoked marijuana in his youth, so you should not take his economic plan seriously." Which fallacy is this?**
   a) Ad Hominem
   b) Straw man argument
   c) Red herring
   d) Slippery slope

<!--t src=e98b385d-->
### 3. **"If we introduce a speed limit on motorways, we will soon be living in a totalitarian state in which every aspect of our lives is regulated." Which fallacy is this?**
   a) Post Hoc Ergo Propter Hoc
   b) False dichotomy
   c) Slippery slope
   d) Argumentum ad Baculum

<!--t src=a988ce21-->
### 4. **Which of the following is a formal fallacy?**
   a) Straw man argument
   b) Affirming the consequent
   c) Argumentum ad Populum
   d) Loaded question

<!--t src=190e9595-->
### 5. **"All parts of this computer are cheap. So the computer is cheap." Which fallacy is present here?**
   a) Division
   b) Composition
   c) Equivocation
   d) Amphiboly

<!--t src=0dce7bae-->
## **Answers:**
1. b) Argumentum ad Ignorantiam
2. a) Ad Hominem
3. c) Slippery slope
4. b) Affirming the consequent
5. b) Composition
